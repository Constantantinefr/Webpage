Anjeo(Aged) this tequila is aged in oak barrel for atleast one year, Anejos are more smooth and have a more developed color than other tequilas Reposado(Rested) These are aged for up to one year the tequila developes a golden hue. Reposados are great to mix with any flavor. Blanco(White) Clear as water, bottled after 50 days of being distilled twice. Blancos can be sipped but are excellnt mixed.
